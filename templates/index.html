CSV Column Requirements

To train the predictive models, the CSV file must contain the following headers:

  • CustomerID - (Optional) Uniquely identifies customers.
  • Age - Age of the customer.
  • Gender - Customer gender (Male / Female / Non-binary).
  • Location - Customer region (e.g. North, South, East, West).
  • Recency - Days since last purchase.
  • Frequency - Number of purchases made.
  • Monetary - Lifetime spend in currency.
  • BrowseTime - Monthly minutes spent on storefront.
  • SupportTickets - Total helpdesk tickets filed.
  • Churn - (Binary 0 or 1) Customer churn classification status.
